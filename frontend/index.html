Technology

About the System

A state-of-the-art AI pipeline combining multiple deep learning architectures for reliable bone density classification.

ResNet-50

A proven image recognition model that has been trained on millions of images, then specially adapted to recognize bone density patterns in knee X-rays.

EfficientNet-B0

A lightweight yet powerful model designed to be highly accurate while using fewer resources, making it ideal for fast and reliable medical image analysis.

DenseNet-121

A powerful model where every layer connects to every other layer, enabling superior feature reuse and making it especially effective at detecting subtle bone density changes in medical images.

Test-Time Augmentation

The system examines your X-ray from multiple angles and orientations, similar to how a doctor would rotate an image, to ensure a more reliable result.

Ensemble Decision

Rather than relying on a single model, all three models vote together on the final result — like getting a second and third opinion for greater confidence.

Risk Assessment

The AI result is combined with patient details like age, sex, and vitamin history to provide a complete risk picture for your clinician to review.
